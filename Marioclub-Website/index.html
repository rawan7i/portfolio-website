It's me, Mario

Super Mario (Japanese: スーパー マリオ, Sōppa Marioh) is a video game series produced by Nintendo and designed by Japanese designer Shigeru Miyamoto. The main character, Mario, is Nintendo's official mascot. Mario is a very short character, wearing his signature blue and red outfit and red hat. He is an Italian plumber living in the Mushroom Kingdom and is known for thwarting evil plans to kidnap Princess Peach and subjugate the Mushroom Kingdom. He is brave, agile, and works closely with his brother Luigi. Mario is one of the most recognizable characters in the world and has appeared in numerous games on Nintendo consoles and other devices such as the Nintendo Switch. Some of the constants in Mario games are enemies. The main enemy is Bowser, a monster resembling a giant turtle. The Goomba is a slow-walking brown mushroom. The Koopa Troopa is a turtle.
